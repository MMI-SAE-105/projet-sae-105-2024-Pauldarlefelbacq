Hi, my name is Paul Darle-Felbacq, I'm 20 and I'm in my first year of a Multimedia and Internet course at the Montbéliard's IUT.

This site was created as part of a project entitled “Préhistoire du Futur”.

The aim of this project is to project a current object either into the past or into the future and imagine what it will be like, its consequences, or what we will think of it with sufficient hindsight.
